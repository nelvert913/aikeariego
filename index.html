YG ARTIST

Yang Hyun-suk founded the worldwide entertainment company YG Entertainment Inc. in 1996. It is based in South Korea. In addition to managing events and producing concerts, the company is a record label, talent agency, music publisher, and music production organization. Furthermore, the business manages many subsidiary businesses under the name YG Plus, a distinct publicly traded company. These businesses include a clothing line, a golf management company, and a cosmetics brand.

SM ARTIST

Lee Soo-man founded the South Korean global entertainment company SM Entertainment Co., Ltd. (Korean: SM엔터테인먼트) in 1995. It is one of the biggest entertainment businesses in South Korea and has helped numerous K-pop stars become well-known worldwide by promoting and popularizing their careers. In addition to managing events and producing concerts, the company is a record label, talent agency, music publisher, and music production organization. The musical side of "Hallyu," often referred to as the "Korean Wave," and the global K-pop craze are credited to SM Entertainment for spearheading early international hits including H.O.T., S.E.S., and BoA.
