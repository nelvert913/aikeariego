NCT DREAM MEMBER'S





NCT DREAM (엔씨티 DREAM) is the 3rd sub-unit of the boy group NCT.
The sub-unit consists of 7 members: Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Chenle, Jaemin, and Jisung.
The group debuted on August 25, 2016 under SM Entertainment.
